Biography

Hello! I'm Emily Johnson, a dedicated law student at the University of Miami School of Law, committed to legal advocacy and public policy. My journey in law is fueled by a passion for justice and a drive to make meaningful changes in society.

I have been fortunate to work on impactful projects such as supporting reproductive rights with Planned Parenthood and addressing human rights issues at the Inter-American Court of Human Rights. When I'm not immersed in my legal studies, I enjoy mentoring youth, advocating for LGBTQ+ equality, and presenting at national and international conferences on topics close to my heart.
